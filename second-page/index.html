What is A.1.?

A.1. is a classic steak sauce known for its tangy, savory flavor. People use it on steaks — burgers, fries, sandwiches, and even marinades.

A.1. is best known as a steak sauce, but it’s used in tons of dishes.

Popular Pairings

  • Steak (ribeye, sirloin, flank)
  • Burgers & sliders
  • Roasted potatoes or fries
  • Meatloaf
  • Steak sandwiches

Classic: steak + A.1. (or use it in a quick pan sauce).

2 Super Easy Uses

1) A.1. Burger Sauce (quick mix)

Mix A.1. + mayo + a little mustard. Spread on buns and add caramelized onions.

2) Tangy Marinade

Mix A.1. + olive oil + garlic + black pepper. Marinate steak 30–60 minutes.
